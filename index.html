How to play

So, sudoku is like a number puzzle where you've got this 9x9 grid divided into smaller 3x3 grids. Your job is to fill in the grid so that each row, column, and 3x3 box contains the numbers 1 through 9. But here's the catch: you can't repeat any numbers in the same row, column, or box. Start with the easier spots and work your way through logically, eliminating possibilities until you fill in the whole grid. That's pretty much the gist of it! Once you get the hang of it, sudoku can be super satisfying and fun to solve.
